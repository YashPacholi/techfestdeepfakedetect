// WHAT ARE DEEPFAKES?
🎭
WHAT IS A DEEPFAKE?
A deepfake is AI-generated media where a person's face or voice is replaced with someone else's using deep learning. The word comes from "deep learning" + "fake".

They are created using GANs (Generative Adversarial Networks) — two AI systems that compete to create increasingly realistic fakes.
🔍
HOW TO SPOT A DEEPFAKE
  • Blurring around hairline and ears
  • Unnatural blinking — too fast or too slow
  • Lip-sync lag — audio slightly off
  • Skin looks "plastic" — no pores
  • Inconsistent lighting on face
  • Jewelry or glasses look distorted
  • Background flickers near face edges
🔊
VOICE DEEPFAKES (AI CLONING)
AI can clone anyone's voice from just 3-10 seconds of audio. Used in scams where "family members" call asking for emergency money.

Red flags: Calls from unknown numbers, urgency, asks for money transfer, slightly robotic tone, no background noise.
🛡️
HOW TO PROTECT YOURSELF
  • Set a secret family code word for emergencies
  • Always call back on known number to verify
  • Ask video callers to cover one eye or turn sideways
  • Never transfer money based on video/voice alone
  • Use DeepGuard AI to verify suspicious media
// CYBER SAFETY TIPS
✅ Passwords: Use 12+ characters with uppercase, numbers, symbols. Use different passwords for every account. Enable 2FA everywhere.
✅ UPI Safety: Never enter your UPI PIN to "receive" money. Always verify merchant name after scanning QR. Banks never ask for PIN.
✅ Email Safety: Hover over links before clicking. Check sender domain carefully. Legitimate banks never ask for OTP via email.
🔴 Never Share: OTP, PIN, CVV, password, Aadhaar number with anyone — even if they claim to be from your bank or government.
🔴 Public WiFi: Never do banking or UPI transactions on public WiFi. Use mobile data or a VPN instead.
🔴 Unknown Apps: Never install apps from links sent via WhatsApp or SMS. Only use Google Play Store or Apple App Store.
